History and Legal Status

Our History

The Rita Shiva Memorial Foundation was established in loving memory of Late Rita Shiva, with a vision to uplift underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and social welfare initiatives.

Since its inception, the foundation has consistently worked toward creating sustainable development opportunities in rural and underserved regions, especially in Bihar and surrounding areas.

Legal Status

Rita Shiva Memorial Foundation is a legally registered non-profit organization operating under the applicable laws of India.

  • ✔ Registered Trust / Society under Indian Law
  • ✔ PAN Registered Organization
  • ✔ Bank Account for Transparent Donations
  • ✔ Compliance with Government Regulations

We are committed to transparency, accountability and ethical governance in all our activities.

Our Journey of Impact & Growth

A timeline highlighting the key milestones and achievements of Rita Shiva Memorial Foundation in serving communities.

2018 – Foundation Established

Rita Shiva Memorial Foundation was founded with the mission to support education and healthcare initiatives for underprivileged families in Bihar.

2019 – Education Support Programs

Launched scholarship drives and distributed study materials to rural students to encourage continued learning.

2021 – Healthcare & Awareness Camps

Organized free medical check-up camps and awareness drives focusing on women and child health.

2023 – Community Development Expansion

Expanded outreach programs to additional rural areas, strengthening community engagement and sustainable initiatives.

The Rita Shiva Memorial Foundation is dedicated to uplifting under proviledge communities through educational, health and social initiatives.
